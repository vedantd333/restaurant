About Us

Welcome to Walk N Eat, where food meets passion. Nestled in the heart of Rajkot, our cozy and inviting restaurant is dedicated to providing an unforgettable dining experience. Our mission is simple-to serve delicious, high-quality meals that bring people together.

At Walk N Eat, we believe that great food starts with fresh ingredients. That's why we work closely with local farmers and suppliers to source the best seasonal produce, meats, and seafood available. Whether it's our signature dishes or new seasonal specials, each plate is crafted with love and care to delight your taste buds.

Our culinary team, led by Executive Chef Garwit, brings a wealth of experience and creativity to every dish. From bold flavors to comforting classics, we aim to satisfy every craving while staying true to our commitment to quality and sustainability.

But it's not just about the food-it's about creating an experience. We want you to feel at home when you dine with us. Our friendly staff are here to make sure your visit is nothing short of exceptional, and we hope you leave feeling like part of our family.

Join us for a memorable meal, whether it's a casual lunch with friends, a romantic dinner for two, or a celebratory gathering. We look forward to welcoming you soon!
